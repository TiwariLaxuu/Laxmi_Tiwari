Reward Design for Trading Agents

Switch from raw PnL to risk‑aware rewards (e.g., downside penalties, exposure regularization, and drawdown‑sensitive components). Consider episode‑end bonuses for stability vs. frequent small rewards.

Normalization for Mixed‑Scale Observations

Velocity & distance benefit from scaling to similar ranges (e.g., standardization or min‑max per feature). For online systems, maintain streaming stats or robust clipping windows.

Reliable Backtesting

Include slippage/spread, latency, partial fills, and transaction costs; evaluate with walk‑forward splits and stress scenarios.
